How It Works?

The service is free and always will be!

A Company publish an offer

The offer consists of a trip already planned by the company that is made available on our platform and details all the routes in which the vehicle made available passes (specifying the times, weight to be delivered, booking limits, etc.)

Users search for routes

Our intelligent system processes the user's requests by tracking what could be the most suitable offers for him. Once the reservation has been made, if allowed by the contractor, the renter can cancel the reservation within the established time limits

Automatization

Updates of all availability are managed automatically by the system. In addition, a powerful analytics service is provided to view the trends of your data, obtaining valuable information.
